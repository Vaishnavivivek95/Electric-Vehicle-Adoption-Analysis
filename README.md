# 🚗⚡ Electric Vehicle Adoption Analysis – Washington State

Exploratory data analysis on Electric Vehicle adoption trends using Python | pandas | matplotlib | seaborn | data visualization| PowerBI

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes Electric Vehicle (EV) registrations across Washington State to uncover adoption trends, regional growth patterns, and vehicle eligibility under the Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle (CAFV) program.
The primary goal is to provide a comprehensive, interactive Power BI dashboard that helps policymakers, analysts, and EV enthusiasts understand where, when, and how EV adoption is growing — and which manufacturers and models are driving that growth.
Built entirely in Power BI, the dashboard enables dynamic filtering by vehicle type, make, model, county, and model year — supporting data-driven decisions around EV infrastructure planning and policy.

## 🎯 Business Objectives

Track EV adoption trends over time by model year across Washington State
Identify top counties driving EV adoption
Analyze the share of BEV vs. PHEV vehicle registrations
Assess CAFV (Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle) eligibility across the fleet
Profile EV adoption by vehicle age category and manufacturer (Make/Model)
Enable regional, brand-level, and policy-level monitoring through interactive visuals

## 🧠 Insights & Findings

BEV (Battery Electric Vehicles) dominate registrations, significantly outpacing PHEVs — reflecting a shift toward fully electric mobility
King County leads all counties in EV adoption, consistent with its urban density and higher income demographics
Model Year 2023 and 2022 saw the sharpest spikes in registrations, pointing to accelerated post-pandemic EV uptake
A substantial portion of vehicles are CAFV-eligible, signaling strong policy alignment and incentive effectiveness
Tesla dominates the Make category, with the Model Y and Model 3 as the most registered models
Newer vehicles (0–5 years) form the largest age category, confirming that EV adoption is a recent and accelerating trend


## 🚀 Impact & Conclusion
This dashboard transforms raw EV registration data into a strategic analytical tool.
By mapping adoption patterns geographically, tracking CAFV eligibility, and profiling vehicles by make, model, and age, it empowers stakeholders to:

Plan charging infrastructure in high-density adoption zones
Design targeted incentive programs for underserved counties
Evaluate policy effectiveness via CAFV eligibility rates
Monitor market share shifts across manufacturers over time

The project demonstrates end-to-end Power BI skills — from raw data ingestion and transformation to KPI design, DAX measures, and polished interactive reporting.
